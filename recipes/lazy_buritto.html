Lazy Burrito

Ingredients

  • FLower Tortillas
  • 1Lb ground beef
  • Chopped black olives
  • green chillies
  • 1Lb shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Brown hamburger in a skillet
  2. Cook the flower Tortillas
  3. after hamburger is cooked mix in chillies, cheese, and olives do not stop until cheese is melted
  4. Assemble and enjoy!
